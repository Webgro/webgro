# Copy style

**No em dashes.** Not in copy, not in captions, not in mock data, not in comments. The site-wide voice is plain, blunt, and direct. Em dashes ("—", U+2014) read as AI-polish and have been scrubbed. Do not reintroduce them.

Substitutes when tempted to reach for one:
- Between clauses that could stand alone: use a period.
- Parenthetical aside: use parens `(like this)`.
- Introducing an explanation or list: use a colon.
- Soft pause inside a sentence: use a comma, if and only if it doesn't create a comma splice.

En dashes (`–`, U+2013) are fine for numeric ranges (e.g. `4–8 weeks`, `£8–15k`). Hyphens are fine as hyphens.
